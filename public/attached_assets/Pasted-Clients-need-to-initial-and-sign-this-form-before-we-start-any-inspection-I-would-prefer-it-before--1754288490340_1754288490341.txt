2. Scope and Standards of Inspection
Inspections are performed in accordance with the Arizona Board of Technical Registration Standards of Professional Practice (https://btr.az.gov/resources/standards). Inspections are visual and non-invasive, limited to readily accessible components.
Discretionary professional observations may include:
•	Construction quality or tradecraft
•	Improper installation
•	Visible or suspected moisture intrusion
Not Included in Standard Scope (unless separately contracted):
•	Destructive testing or dismantling of systems
•	Formal code enforcement or building permit research
•	Concealed conditions behind walls, ceilings, or underground
•	Pest, radon, asbestos, or mold testing involving lab analysis
Due to licensure and trade experience, some services may be available upon request under a separate agreement.

4. Discretionary Observations Based on Licensure

•	Use thermal imaging and moisture detection devices

CLIENT understands these services are not mandated under the AZ Home Inspector Standards but are offered as added value.
